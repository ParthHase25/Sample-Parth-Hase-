FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our architectural services and process.

What is the typical timeline for a residential project?

+

The timeline varies based on project scope. Typically, design phase takes 6-8 weeks, approvals 4-6 weeks, and construction 8-12 months for a complete home. We provide detailed timelines during initial consultation.

Do you handle building permits and approvals?

+

Yes, we handle all necessary building permits, approvals, and regulatory compliance. Our team is well-versed with local building codes and municipal requirements in Pune.

What are your fees for architectural services?

+

Our fees depend on project scope and complexity. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden costs. Design fees typically range from 8-12% of construction cost. Contact us for a detailed quote.

Do you provide 3D visualizations?

+

Absolutely! We provide detailed 3D visualizations and virtual walkthroughs to help you visualize your project before construction begins. This ensures you're completely satisfied with the design.

Can you work within a specific budget?

+

Yes, we work closely with clients to design within their budget constraints. We provide value engineering solutions and material alternatives to optimize costs without compromising on quality.
